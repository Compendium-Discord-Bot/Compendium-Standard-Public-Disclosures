# Credit Issuance Policy

## I. Definition

Credit, or credits, refers to money that is maintained, in trust, with Compendium, and can only be spent on Compendium products and services. It operates similarly to a gift card.

## II. Issuance and Access

Credit is issued in association with Discord user accounts, and remains associated to these accounts, unless and until the user either dispenses with their credit through redeeming their credit with Compendium products or services, refunds their credit, dispenses with their credit through donation, or loses access to their credit through adverse circumstances.

1. Chooses to dispense with their credit by conducting a one-time refund *(See §III. (1) below)*;
2. Donates their credit to Compendium for promotional usage *(See §III. (2) below)*;
3. Voids their access to credit by:
   1. Losing access to their Discord account without option of recovery;
   2. Losing access to Compendium through a serious breach of Terms of Service

## III. How credit is used

Credit can be redeemed with Compendium products and services only By default, when any balance of credit exists, it will be the default tender used in any transaction with Compendium, until the balance is depleted. The remaining balance not paid by tender will be paid by the payment method on file.

## III. Transferrability

Credit with Compendium may be transferred in a number of ways. Note that all transfers of credit are final and cannot be reversed.

### 1. One-Time Refunds

If you have a credit balance and want your credit refunded, you are entitled to have your entire credit refunded to you exactly once under each of the following circumstances:

1. You can still access your Discord user profile and wish to no longer be a subscriber with Compendium, OR;
2. You can no londer access your Discord account, your account with Compendium has no serious violations recorded, and wish to recover any remaining funds *(See IV. (1) for more information)*.

### 2. Promotional Usage Transfer

If you have a credit balance, but wish to participate in a promotional offering by Compendium, such as receiving a discount for sponsoring prospective members for premium subscriptions, you may do so with credit, provided that no promotional terms are breached in the process.

## IV. Voids of Credit

There are circumstances where credit will be voided, or erased, with Compendium. We prefer that these circumstances be avoided entirely, but they are explained below for the purposes of transparency, in hopes that none of these circumstances occur.

### 1. Permanent loss of Discord Account

Because credit is attatched to a Discord user account, losing access to this account makes it harder for us to validate who possessed the credit before the account was banned. Because of this, we require that you contact us via our email at support@`[WIP - Website domain]` including the following:

1. Your intent in seeking a refund;
2. The Discord User ID for the user linked to the subscription;
3. `[WIP - Some other identifier to prove who you say you are.]`

### 2. Termination of Account

If your account is terminated with Compendium, any credit balance maintained at the point of termination is forfeited to the Service to be used at the discretion of the Service. Any active subscriptions will also automatically be cancelled so you are not billed.
