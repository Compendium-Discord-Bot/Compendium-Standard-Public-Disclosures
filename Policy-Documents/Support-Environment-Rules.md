# Support Environment Rules

These rules govern both the support community, and all interactions conducted by any of our email-based support channels, and are published both on the Compendium Support Hub's [#rules](https://discord.com/channels/1232458290563387392/1232458290601136150 "Rules – Compendium Support Hub") channel, and on the [public disclosures repository](Policy-Documents/Support-Environment-Rules.md "Support Environment Rules – Compendium Standard Public Disclosures").

---

**Support is a privilege, not a right** – Violation of these rules may diminish or eliminate your ability to seek or receive support.
