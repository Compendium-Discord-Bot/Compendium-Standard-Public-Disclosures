# Support Environment Rules

These rules govern both the support community, and all interactions conducted by any of our email-based support channels, and are published both on the Compendium Support Hub's [#rules](https://discord.com/channels/1232458290563387392/1232458290601136150 "Rules – Compendium Support Hub") channel, and on the [public disclosures repository](https://github.com/Compendium-Discord-Bot/Compendium-Standard-Public-Disclosures/blob/main/Policy-Documents/Support-Environment-Rules.md "Support Environment Rules – Compendium Standard Public Disclosures"), and as applicable determined by the nature of the support channel used.

---

**Support is a privilege, not a right** - Violation of these rules may diminish or eliminate your ability to seek or receive support.

---

## I. Community Rules

1. The mission of our support environments is to provide support for [Compendium](https://discord.com/users/1153092754310692935 "Compendium – Main Bot"), to you to resolve errors, assist with setup, and answer any questions about Compendium. This requires cooperation and intention to solve the problem at hand. All rules flow from the following: *Do not undermine the mission of this support environment.*
2. Follow the [TOS](https://discord.com/terms "Terms of Service – Discord") and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines "Community Guidelines – Discord") of Discord, the [TOS](https://github.com/Compendium-Discord-Bot/Compendium-Standard-Public-Disclosures/blob/main/Policy-Documents/Terms-of-Service.md "Terms of Service – Compendium Standard Public Disclosures") of Compendium, and all applicable laws governing your behavior while interacting on Discord.
3. You are not required to treat everyone with any high honors, but grant everyone basic dignity.
4. Do not harass or abuse any member of this server. Do not use discriminatory or foul language.
5. Presume good faith and investigate before jumping to conclusions. Even if someone is being awful, do not engage with them, report it to us in `[WIP - procedure]` and move on.
6. Absolutely no spamming or advertisement of any kind is allowed. If your account is hacked to advertise or spam, advertisement or spam still occurred. Please take steps to secure your account and do not click on malicious links so you are not banned from the support server.
7. This server is a strictly SFW environment.
8. Do not ping people for no reason. Do not ping support staff unless they've already engaged you in replies *(One ping is okay, more if it's been > 48 hours without a response)*.
9. Do not send links unless requested by a member of staff.

## II. Support Rules

We aim to provide the best support in a reasonable amount of time, however, support is a partnership, therefore we require you to help us, to help you. Thanks in advance for respecting our boundaries and for being a good partner in support.

1. If you can't explain something, it's okay to admit it, but please do your best to describe what's wrong or confusing. Topics containing little more than "help me", "it's not working", "it's broken", "getting an error", "i need help", or "i have a question" will be unceremoniously closed, since it helps no one.
2. Provide necessary context. Explain what steps you took leading up to the cause of your support need. Explain *(or include screenshots of)* what you see, and what any error or warning says. Without context *(as in #1 of this section)*, we will be spending time fishing for context that could have been provided at the beginning.
3. Compendium's configuration relies heavily upon knowledge of how Discord works, from familiarity of roles, permissions, and channels, and how they interact with one another. Questions about how Compendium extends these concepts are perfectly fine, but we require a reasonable baseline familiarity of how Discord implements of these controls, and recommend having good knowledge of these concepts before using Compendium.
4. Involving your applications between Compendium as a service provider, and various servers which handle your submissions, please recognize the difference between an error in the service and an inconvenience of the people handling your review that you don't like. We are happy to address issues with our service, but please don't ask us about things outside of our control.
5. Do not initiate a help request by pinging anyone. It's rude. We aim to help you as soon as we can. A ping reply on topic with an existing conversation is fine. A ping after 3 days of not getting a response is also fine.
